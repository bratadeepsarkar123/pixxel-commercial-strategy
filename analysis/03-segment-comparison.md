- **Weights:** From PS mandate — market size, WTP, competition, regulatory framework
- **Regulatory weight elevated** for India B2G context (30%)

| Willingness to pay | 25% | Margin potential & pricing power |

## Recommendation

### Primary vertical: **PMFBY / Crop Insurance Index Underwriting (Segment B)**

**Rationale (4 points):**

1. **Larger addressable pool** — $72M vs $30M sub-TAM (71% of total); aligns with national scale of 4.19 cr enrolled farmers.
2. **Regulatory tailwind** — YES-TECH 30% mandatory technology weight + PMFBY's stated use of remote sensing (S021, S027) creates procurement pull.
3. **Strategic fit with EO PPP** — 12-satellite national constellation explicitly includes agriculture applications (S001, S003); Pixxel's govt mandate is to serve Indian users first.
4. **Consortium channel** — Partner with SatSure (analytics/incumbent) for YES-TECH compliance while Pixxel supplies **hyperspectral data layer** — avoids head-on product competition.

### Why not enterprise-first?

- Enterprise segment offers **higher WTP per account** but **longer B2B sales cycles**, heavier Planet/drone competition, and **smaller hectare pool**.
- Pixxel needs **FY2026–27 revenue proof** while Firefly is operational but national constellation is still deploying — PMFBY state-level contracts provide **faster volume**.

### Expansion sequence FY2026–28

| Phase | Period | Focus | Target | Revenue mix |
|-------|--------|-------|--------|-------------|
| **Phase 1 — Prove** | FY2026 | PMFBY pilots | 2 states, 3 crops, YES-TECH-aligned yield indices | 70% B2G insurance / 30% enterprise |
| **Phase 2 — Scale** | FY2027 | PMFBY expansion + enterprise beachhead | 6 states; 10 enterprise accounts (sugar, horticulture FPOs) | 60% insurance / 40% enterprise |
| **Phase 3 — Margins** | FY2028 | Aurora SaaS upsell | National EO live; 25 enterprise accounts; 8-state PMFBY | 50% insurance / 50% enterprise SaaS |

### Enterprise precision ag role

**Secondary but strategic** — target high-value hyperspectral use cases (horticulture, nutrient stress, export grapes) where Pixxel's 135+ band differentiation commands premium SaaS pricing. Sahyadri-scale FPOs are archetype accounts (S033).
