# Issue Tree — Pixxel Commercial Strategy

> **Phase 1 complete** | Method: MECE issue tree + hypothesis-driven prioritization (see `research/methodology-notes.md`)

---

## Problem restatement

**How should Pixxel prioritize agricultural verticals and monetization models to reach profitable, scalable commercial revenue in India by FY2028, given its hyperspectral constellation, Aurora analytics platform, and national EO PPP mandate?**

---

## Hypothesis (testable)

**PMFBY satellite index underwriting should be Pixxel's primary FY2026–28 revenue pillar, with enterprise precision-ag SaaS as the margin-accretive expansion lane — delivered through a SaaS-led hybrid monetization model that bundles Aurora analytics with standardized imagery.**

| Test | If true, hypothesis holds | If false, pivot to |
|------|---------------------------|-------------------|
| PMFBY procurement pool > enterprise precision-ag pool by FY2028 | YES-TECH scale + ₹35,951 cr scheme budget creates larger addressable contract value | Enterprise precision ag as primary |
| Pixxel can win PMFBY share without margin destruction | DaaS/SaaS hybrid achieves >55% blended gross margin | Pure enterprise SaaS |
| Regulatory tailwind accelerates satellite adoption | YES-TECH mandatory weight + national EO constellation agriculture mandate | Delay PMFBY; focus enterprises |

## MECE issue tree (3 layers)

**ROOT:** How should Pixxel achieve profitable commercial scale in India agriculture by FY2028?

```
ROOT: Profitable India ag commercialization by FY2028
│
├── [★1] MARKET ATTRACTIVENESS — Is the opportunity large enough?
│   ├── 1a. TAM: Satellite-based precision ag analytics (India)
│   │   ├── Bottom-up: farmable ha → addressable % → adoption → $/ha
│   │   └── Top-down: Ag GVA × digital analytics spend %
│   ├── 1b. Growth: Precision ag & satellite imagery CAGR
│   └── 1c. Timing: FY2026–28 catalysts (Digital Ag Mission, YES-TECH, EO PPP)
│
├── [★2] VERTICAL PRIORITIZATION — Which segment to enter first?
│   ├── 2a. Precision Agriculture Analytics (B2B enterprise / FPO / agribusiness)
│   │   ├── Addressable hectares & account count
│   │   ├── Willingness to pay (yield ROI, input savings)
│   │   ├── Competition (Planet, drones, ISRO Bhuvan, domestic startups)
│   │   └── Regulatory ease (market-driven procurement)
│   └── 2b. Crop Insurance / PMFBY Index Underwriting (B2B/B2G)
│       ├── Insured area & farmer enrollment scale
│       ├── WTP (insurer/govt budget per insurance unit)
│       ├── Competition (SatSure, MNCFC/ISRO stack, incumbents)
│       └── Regulatory (YES-TECH, CCE optimization, PMFBY guidelines)
│
├── [★3] MONETIZATION & UNIT ECONOMICS — DaaS vs SaaS vs hybrid?
│   ├── 3a. DaaS: Raw / ARD imagery pricing ($/km²), margin, scalability
│   ├── 3b. SaaS: Aurora subscription + tokens, margin, CAC, LTV
│   ├── 3c. Customer concentration & channel risk (govt vs enterprise)
│   └── 3d. Blended revenue mix trajectory FY2026 → FY2028
│
└── [4] GO-TO-MARKET & REGULATORY (enabler — feeds scoring, not primary analysis)
    ├── 4a. EO PPP constellation timeline (12 satellites, FY2027+ ops)
    ├── 4b. IN-SPACe policy & data sovereignty requirements
    ├── 4c. Sales motion: enterprise ABM vs govt/insurer tenders
    └── 4d. Consortium dynamics (SatSure, Dhruva, PierSight partnership/channel)
```

**Breakdown method by level:**
- Level 1: **Segment** (market → vertical → monetization → GTM)
- Level 2: **Math** where quantifiable (TAM formula; unit economics)
- Level 3: **Stakeholder** for competition/regulatory sub-branches
